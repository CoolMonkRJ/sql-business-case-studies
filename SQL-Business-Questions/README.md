 SQL for Business Questions

**Dataset:** Northwind  
**Tools:** MySQL  

### Business Scenarios Covered
1. Identified top-performing product categories by revenue using GROUP BY and SUM
2. Analyzed average order value by customer region
3. Ranked customers by lifetime value
4. Identified inactive customers using LEFT JOIN and date filtering

### Skills Demonstrated
- GROUP BY with aggregations (SUM, AVG, COUNT)
- INNER JOIN and LEFT JOIN
- Subqueries for order-level aggregation
- Translating business questions into SQL queries

### Business Impact
These queries replicate real-world analytics tasks such as revenue analysis, customer segmentation, and churn identification.
